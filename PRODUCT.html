Our Products & Solutions

Water Treatment Systems

Ozone System

The Ozone system reduces organic matter, COD/BOD load, disinfects odour & colour, and oxidizes pollutants effectively.

Ultrafiltration (UF)

Separates bacteria, viruses, and colloids with a compact design, stable output, and minimal chemical use.

Water Treatment Plant (WTP)

Multi-stage treatment including coagulation, sedimentation, filtration, and disinfection for safe drinking water.

Demineralization (DM) Plant

Removes dissolved minerals and impurities, ensuring high efficiency and reliability for industrial needs.

Reverse Osmosis (RO) Plant

Removes up to 99% of contaminants with high-performance membranes, compact design, and smart monitoring.

Water Softener

Removes hardness-causing minerals via ion exchange resin for longer equipment life and efficient operation.

Wastewater Treatment

Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

MBBR, SBR, MBR, and FAB technologies for industrial wastewater treatment and regulatory compliance.

Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

ASP, MBBR, MBR, Electro-Coagulation, and SBR tech for compact, efficient sewage treatment.

Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF)

Clarifies wastewater by floating suspended matter with micro-bubbles. Used in dairy, oil, textile industries.

Primary & Secondary Clarifier

Removes suspended solids using gravity settling with mechanical scrapers for sludge & scum.

Wet Fume Scrubber

Neutralizes corrosive gases, absorbs particulates, and handles high-temperature streams efficiently.

Multi Effect Evaporator (MEE)

Reduces energy consumption while maximizing evaporation efficiency in industrial applications.

Chemicals

Chemicals

We supply antiscalants, membrane cleaners, bio-cultures, odor removers, and ETP/STP treatment chemicals.

Environmental Monitoring

Environmental Monitoring

Ambient air & noise monitoring, stack emission analysis, ventilation surveys, and ETP/STP sample testing.
